About Koffura UF

Koffura UF is a student-run business dedicated to creating small-batch products that combine functionality with sustainable materials. Our team focuses on careful sourcing, responsible production, and giving back to our community.

Our Mission

We want to create products that last, reduce waste, and support ethical practices. Every product is designed with purpose and crafted to be useful in daily life.

Who We Are

We're a small team of students learning entrepreneurship, design, and production. Koffura UF stands for quality, responsibility, and creativity.
